The following contains a recipe for fettucine alfredo!

Fettucine Alfredo is one of my childhood staples so the following recipe is one which anyone can recreate and enjoy for themselves!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. fettuccine noodles
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • salt and pepper, depends on your preference
  • 2 cups of freshly grated parmesan cheese

Cooking Directions:

  1. Cook the pasta based on the duration and steps on the back of the pasta wrap/container in which you purchased it
  2. Warm up the butter and heavy cream in a saucepan or skillet. Season the skillet with however much salt and pepper you prefer
  3. While your pasta is cooking, grate the parmesan cheese into another bowl and poor the warm butter and cream over it
  4. Once the pasta has finished cooking, pour out the excess water and put the pasta in the bowl of sauce and mix it well
  5. Sample the food and add more seasoning if need or possibly add in some extra pasta water if sauce is too thick
  6. Put the pasta into a serving bowl and grab a drink, appetizers, or any add-ons such as chicken, shrimp, etc. and enjoy your meal!
